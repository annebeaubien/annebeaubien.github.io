Favorite food blogs and websites:

Never Seconds - A nine year old girl photographs her school lunches in Scotland and brings world wide attention to the school food crisis.

Tuesday Recipe - Tori Ritchie’s recipe blog, updated with new recipes (almost) every tuesday.

Simply Recipes - Elise Bauer shares hundreds of her family recipes in this food blog.

Norwegian Food Illustrated by an Italian - An Italian graphic designer living in Norway and interpreting Norwegian people and food in illustrations. (Written in English.)

Jamie Oliver’s Food Revolution - A British chef’s attempt to save lives by changing the way people eat.

Nordic Nibbler - A British guy, married to a Norwegian girl, living in Norway writes about restaurants and food in Scandinavia, Finland and Iceland.

School Lunch Roundup - 100 days of real food, a blog about cutting out processed food.
